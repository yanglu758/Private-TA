Why Choose Us?

Here are some benefits you will get.

Tutor: Part-Time Tuition Career

Short of pocket money? Trouble getting a decent and well-paid part-time job? Tired of finding your students as a part-time tutor? Here is a great opportunity for you! Simply make your post on our platform and you will be contacted by interested students in no time!

Tutor: Strengthening Concepts

Confucius said:"One is worthy of being or becoming a teacher if one is able to derive new understanding while revising old." Right! Teaching as a tutor is not only helping others, but also yourself. Revising your past concepts will help you strengthen your understanding and gain new insights.

Tutee: Instant Enlightenment

Having troubles understanding the challenging concepts? Shy of turning to your TA or lecturer for help? Need immediate one-to-one help session? No worries, help is on the way! We offer many choices of competent tutors for you! Simply search for your most ideal tutor and contact him! Price is negotiable!

Tutee: CAP Booster Richard was here :)

Want to challenge the bell-curve god and be the new face on the Dean's List? Not a unrealistic dream! Consistent help from your tutor definitely will enhance your understanding and improve your grades! Gone are the painful days of getting stuck at one problem for hours and feeling desperate and helpless!
